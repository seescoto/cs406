Home

This website is a fun, easy way to encode and decode messages and learn a little more about basic cryptographic schemes used both in the past and today! With a 'key' (a password or phrase that both you and a friend can remember) and this site, you can send secret messages without fear of being overheard.

Learn more about how your data is kept safe through the beginner-friendly explanations at the bottom of each scheme's page, or dive deeper into the mathematical reasoning behind how and why a scheme works with a more advanced discussion below that.

Happy encoding!
